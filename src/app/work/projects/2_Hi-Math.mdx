## Overview
In this project, as the team lead, I was responsible for designing the core framework—specifically an editor tool—that enabled other developers to build levels efficiently. I collaborated with management to assess workloads and allocate hours based on individual skill sets and overall team capacity. Additionally, I coordinated between the design and development teams to ensure all optimization requirements were met.
## Key Features

- **Architecture Guidelines**: Created a dynamic system architecture to handle multilevel development and protyping in fast pace without putting much pressure on the team.
- **IAP model**: Developed multiplayer features, including playing with friends, room codes, player invites, and quick chat, to enhance the social aspect of the game.
- **Documentation and Usage Guidelines**: Developed comprehensive documentation to showcase components, usage patterns, and best practices, ensuring the design system is easy to adopt by other teams.

## Technologies Used

- **Unity**: Game development.
- **Github**: Version control.
- **Visual Studios**:  IDE.
- **Figma**: Design.
- **Azure boards**: Boards management.


## Challenges and Learnings
The key challenge on this project was to identify the proper material for autism. And architecturing the system that would generate multi levels instantly with very flexible and dynamic approach.

## Outcome
The game is released on google playstore.
